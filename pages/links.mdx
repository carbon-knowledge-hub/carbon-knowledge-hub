# Links

A collection of useful links to external resources on carbon trading

## Reports and data

- [Rainforest Finance: How to Make $500 Billion Flow](https://about.bnef.com/blog/rainforest-finance-how-to-make-500-billion-flow/)
  BloombergNEF opinion piece on rainforest finance.
- [Climate Policy Factbook: COP27 Edition]([https://about.bnef.com/blog/bloombergnef-announces-its-first-net-zero-summit-in-bali-ahead-of-b20-and-g20-summits/](https://about.bnef.com/blog/2021-support-for-fossil-fuels-by-g-20-nations-reached-highest-level-since-2014/))
  BloombergNEF assessment of G-20 countries' policies on carbon pricing, fossil-fuel support and climate-risk disclosure.
- [From Aspirations to Action: Unpacking the Results of our Global Net-Zero Survey](https://www.nortonrosefulbright.com/en/knowledge/publications/0ab4e6c1/from-aspirations-to-action-unpacking-the-results-of-our-global-netzero-survey)
  Analysis of survey by Norton Rose Fulbright and BloombergNEf on how organizations engage with carbon markets on their pathways to net-zero.
- [Carbon Market Business Briefs](https://ieta.org/carbonmarketbusinessbriefs)
  IETA's business briefs on carbon markets and taxes.
- [Interactive Emissions-Trading Scheme Map](https://icapcarbonaction.com/en/ets)
  ICAP's interactive map with information on each program.
- [Carbon Pricing Dashboard](https://carbonpricingdashboard.worldbank.org/)
  World Bank's tool to visualize carbon markets and taxes around the world.

## Initiatives

- [Integrity Council for the Voluntary Carbon Market](https://icvcm.org/)
  To set and enforce global standards so high-quality carbon credits efficiently mobilize finance toward mitigation and climate-resilienct development.
- [Internation Carbon Reduction & Offset Alliance](https://www.icroa.org/)
  To develop best practices in emissions reductions and offsetting.
- [Markets for Natural Climate Solutions](https://ncs.ieta.org/)
  To maximize business investment in natural climate solutions.
- [Science Based Targets initiative](https://sciencebasedtargets.org/)
  To enable organizations to set science-based emissions reduction targets.
- [Taskforce on Scaling Voluntary Carbon Markets](https://www.iif.com/tsvcm)
  To scale an effective and efficient voluntary carbon market.
- [Voluntary Carbon Markets Integrity Initiative](https://vcmintegrity.org/about/)
  To drive participation in voluntary carbon markets.
